## Hello — I'm Jihad 👋

I'm a Principal Software Engineer at **Foothill Technology Solutions**, working across **Restaurant365's** engineering organization from Nablus, Palestine 🇵🇸

I design the seams between teams and systems: service boundaries, data contracts, integration patterns, and the platform capabilities that let products evolve safely. My path from backend intern to principal in under four years has been shaped by taking ownership of increasingly difficult, cross-team problems.

Today, I:

- architect and evolve business-critical distributed backend systems;
- lead a weekly architecture council spanning six engineering teams;
- turn recurring engineering friction into reusable platform capabilities; and
- explore how agentic AI can improve investigation, diagnostics, and developer workflows.
